LexRAG | Intelligent Legal Document QA

LexRAG

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework for intelligent legal document question answering using Indian legal texts. The system combines semantic retrieval with Large Language Models to deliver accurate, context-aware, and grounded legal responses.

Project Overview

LexRAG is an AI-powered legal question answering system designed to overcome the limitations of traditional keyword-based legal search. Instead of relying solely on exact keyword matching, the framework employs semantic search using transformer embeddings and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to understand the intent and context of legal queries. The system processes Indian legal documents—including the Constitution of India, IPC, CrPC, and BNSS—into semantic vector representations, enabling efficient retrieval of relevant legal provisions even when the user's query does not contain matching keywords. Retrieved legal context is supplied to a Large Language Model to generate factually grounded and contextually relevant responses while minimizing hallucinations.

Key Features

Semantic Legal Search

  • Retrieves relevant legal provisions based on semantic similarity rather than exact keyword matching.
  • Improves retrieval accuracy for complex legal queries.
  • Understands contextual relationships within legal documents.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation

  • Combines semantic retrieval with Large Language Models.
  • Generates grounded responses using retrieved legal evidence.
  • Reduces hallucinations by providing factual legal context.

Efficient Vector Retrieval

  • Documents are segmented into semantic chunks.
  • Chunks are converted into dense vector embeddings.
  • Fast similarity search through vector indexing.

Legal Knowledge Base

  • Constitution of India
  • Indian Penal Code (IPC)
  • Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC)
  • Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS)

Experimental Results

  • Generated approximately 1,799 semantic legal chunks from Indian legal documents.
  • Improved semantic retrieval compared to traditional keyword-based search.
  • Successfully retrieved relevant legal provisions even when exact query terms were absent.
  • Produced context-aware, grounded, and factually consistent legal responses.
  • Reduced hallucination through Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
  • Validated across constitutional rights, criminal procedures, and IPC-related legal queries.

Project Highlights

Problem Addressed

Traditional legal search engines struggle with contextual understanding, making it difficult to locate relevant legal information using natural language queries.

Solution

LexRAG leverages semantic embeddings, vector search, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation to provide accurate, explainable, and context-aware legal question answering.

Impact

The framework demonstrates the practical applicability of AI-driven legal assistance systems for researchers, legal professionals, and students seeking reliable legal information.
